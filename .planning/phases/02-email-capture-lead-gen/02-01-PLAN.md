<objective>
Implement the email gate trigger mechanism that blocks full access to search results after the user's 3rd search. This plan establishes the core gating logic: search count persistence in localStorage, gate overlay UI, and state management linking the two.

Purpose: Enable freemium conversion by showing results are available but gating them behind email signup, creating natural urgency at search #3 (when user has proven intent).

Output: Working gate overlay that appears on 3rd search, persists across sessions, and can be unlocked after email verification.
</objective>
